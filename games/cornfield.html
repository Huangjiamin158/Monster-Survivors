Game Description

Cornfield is a pulse-pounding puzzle-survival game where you must outsmart a relentless corn monster. Navigate a shadowy maze filled with complex challenges, testing your strategy and nerves. Solve puzzles quickly and carefully to find the exit before the terrifying creature catches up and becomes their meal. Every step could be your last in this heart-pounding escape adventure.

Game Controls

WASD/Arrows → Move
LMB/E → Interact/Pick-up
RMB/G → Drop item
C → Crouch
H → Get a hint
F → Fire
Shift → Sprint
Tab → Pause
